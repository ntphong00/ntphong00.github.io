Swipe Delay Impact Evaluation

Goal: This evaluation aims to measure the impact of swipe delay on Quality of Experience in short video service. During the evaluation, you will watch multiple test sample, each consists of a list of six short videos. You can go to next video in the list anytime by swipe down on your screen. Delay might occur when swiping and in that case you will see a black screen for a period of time. At the end of each session, you will be asked to rate the quality of the test sample on a 5-point scale as follows.
  1. Poor
  2. Bad
  3. Fair
  4. Good
  5. Excellent
Procedure:
  1. Click the "Start Training Session" button below to start a training session. In the training session, you will be showing several test samples in which delay after each video will be displayed. You can also practice choosing score for each test sample. At the end of the training session, you will be directed to the main evaluation.
  2. At the beginning of the main evaluation session, you will be asked to provide your name, age, and experience with short video service. The main evaluation session consists of total 25 test samples. The evaluation time is approximately 30 minutes. At the end of the evaluation, please download a log file containing all of your evaluation scores and upload it to the specified drive folder.
